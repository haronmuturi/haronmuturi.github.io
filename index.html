Profile Summary

A dedicated Civil Engineer with 2+ years of progressive experience in building construction, site supervision, and infrastructure development. Proven track record in the successful commissioning of the Chuka Modern Market, a multi-million shilling Affordable Housing Program project under the State Department of Housing & Urban Development. Skilled in designing and supervising civil works including drainage systems, roads, and water supply networks. Proficient in reading engineering drawings, structural detailing, materials testing, and quality control procedures. Experienced in engineering surveys, setting out, levelling, and using AutoCAD for design support.

Throughout my career, I have worked on diverse projects ranging from water pipeline installations at MEWASSCO to box culvert construction for the Kenya Rural Roads Authority (KeRRA). My technical expertise includes BOQ review, progress reporting, compliance monitoring, and ensuring adherence to design standards and specifications. I am passionate about building sustainable infrastructure that makes a real difference in communities, and I bring strong team collaboration, communication, and problem-solving skills to every project I undertake.
